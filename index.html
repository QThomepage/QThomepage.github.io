Biography

Henry Ma

Professor, Doctoral Supervisor, Department Head

Director, Institute of Data Science & Intelligent Decision

Deputy Director, AI & Data Science Application Laboratory

Dr. Henry Ma is Professor and Department Head in Department of Information Management and Decision Sciences in School of Business and Management at Cambridge University, who has received his Ph.D in Management in 2013 and the bachelor degree in Information Management and Information Systems in 2007 both from Cambridge University. He is now Director of Institute of Data Science & Intelligent Decision and Deputy Director of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Application Lab. His research interests focus on mobile big data analytics, business analytics and decision-making, e-commerce and search services as well as policy informatics. Dr. Henry Madison has published over 20 research papers, several of which have been published in main-stream IS international academic journals.
